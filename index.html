_Slow Bar.

is an innovative music collective, record label & a global podcast series dedicated to the slow & beautiful side to electronic music. we are based in germany, california, mexico, and georgia, and support artists & eclectic music from all over the world. join our journey!

_ecstatic dance journeys

we love to dance, & beyond our club gigs, we love creating immersive dance journeys. invite you to join us on a journey through diverse genres & layers of music from around the world - a celebration of dance, emotion, & connection. in the safe space we create together, everything is welcome to arise. free from substances, conversation, and phones, this is a space to fully immerse yourself in the experience.
